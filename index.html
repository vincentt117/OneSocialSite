About the App

One Social is a revolutionary new app that has the ability to change the world of social media as you know it. With the capability to add someone on multiple platforms instantly, this app has the potential to become a worldwide sensation. Allowing you to follow, like and add a new acquaintance through different channelsall at once, can seamlessly improve connections leading to better and longer lasting relationships. One Social, is a one stop app that will allow you to expand your reach on social media exponentially.

By simply getting your new acquaintance’s phone number or photo, you can send them links to your Facebook, Twitter, Snapchat and Instagram accounts right away (or any other accounts you may have). This means another thing for you, more followers, more friends, and more likes!

Why use ONE?

We’ve all used facial recognition software, either to put Bambi ears on grandma at Thanksgiving dinner, or send a cute doggie face to you’re love interest (or send the grossest face possible to you’re BFFL, we’re not here to judge). Imagine using such ground-breaking technology to add someone to your social network. By taking a photo with the person you just met, The One can recognize your new friend based on their mug and send them a friend request on any app you choose.

Don't like facial recognition? Don't worry! We can provide QR Codes (yeah those weird Blackberry square barcodes) and text message options.

Controlled Privacy

The One is the social app you’ve been waiting for. We require no access to your information and have security policies that only allow others to view your profiles, if and when you let them.

No matter the situation you’re in, you can change your profile settings to let those you meet add you only on the combination of social media platforms you desire, instantly.

Out meeting people at the bar? You can configure your profile with ease to add your new friends on snapchat and insta so they can follow your crazy party stories.

At a networking event meeting potential new employers? Change your profile settings to only add new contacts on linked-in and twitter at once, so they can follow your professional career progression and not see the video of you shot gunning a hard lemonade with your friends.

Just met someone cute at the gym? Change your profile to add them on facebook and Insta so they can see your blurry gym mirror selfies, showing off that rockin’ bod.
